ASTROHEAD, is the brand new font family of the Headfonts collection.  This geometric sans serif typeface takes its inspiration from the early desktop systems, and video games. This style approach gives it a distinct futuristic, old sci-fi movie titles look. Astrohead has a minimal design, with wide letterforms, a high X-height, closed apretures, short "e" finial and its overall design mixes round and sharp corners to enhance that futuristic appearance. Astrohead is the perfect choice for ultramodern designs.  From video game designs, sci-fi inspired posters and movie titles to logos but can also be used for small articles and occasional texts.
